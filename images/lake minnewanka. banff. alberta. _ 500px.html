Photographing Landscapes at Twilight: Golden Hour & Beyond

1.299

This Class will inspire you to chase the sun to create epic landscape photographs. With the help of photographer Tanner Wendell Stewart, youll learn camera techniques to prepare you for the ever-changing light conditions of the golden hour. In his video tutorial, Tanner demonstrates camera techniques for shooting in low light, how to frame images for composites, and more. This is the first in a series of three Classes aimed at photographers in search of their next amazing landscape. The second Class will focus on capturing the magic that happens after sunset, when the sky comes alive with stars. Then, Tanner will follow-up with a third lesson in Photoshop, teaching photographers how to retouch and composite photos theyve created throughout the course.
